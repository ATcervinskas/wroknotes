Fundamental analysis in crypto

💡 Since cryptocurrencies lack traditional financial reports, FA in crypto involves:  
✅ **Project Whitepapers** – Understanding a project's vision & technology.  
✅ **Tokenomics** – Supply, demand, and staking models.  
✅ **Developer Activity** – Frequent updates show project commitment.  
✅ **Adoption & Partnerships** – Real-world use cases increase value.  
✅ **On-Chain Metrics** – Network activity, transaction volume, and wallet growth
